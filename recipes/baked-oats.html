Strawberry Banana Blended Baked Oats

Home

Description

These strawberry banana blended baked oats are easy to make, kid friendly, and packed with healthy ingredients. Everything comes together in a blender and bakes up into a perfectly portioned, delicious treat that tastes just like having cake for breakfast!

ingredients

    1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats 1 overripe banana 2 large eggs 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk or milk of choice 2 tablespoons honey or sweetener of choice 1 teaspoon baking powder 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon 1/8 teaspoon salt 1/3 cup chopped fresh strawberries 1/4 cup chocolate chips (optional)
